About us

We are a local, family run business located downtown Harriston, ON on Main Street. The One Stop Christmas Shop opened in 1955 and was originally run by Mr. and Mrs. Kringle before they passed it on to their children. Those owners eventually passed it down to their kids who run it today. Open seasonally from September to February, our store has everything you could need for Christmas! We are open six days a week, so come down and check us out!
