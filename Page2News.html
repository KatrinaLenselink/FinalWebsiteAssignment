Santa Visit

https://timedotcom.files.wordpress.com/2015/12/mall-santa.jpg?quality=85

It's that time of year again! Santa will be visiting our store this year on December 4th! Make sure to have your list prepared to tell Santa what you want for Christmas this year! Each child will get a free candy cane after sitting on Santa's knee. Photos will be taken upon request and we would appreciate a donation for our food drive in exchange for photos. You don't want to miss out on this Christmas opportunity!

Christmas Trees

https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7152/6484959711_5bb9113ca7_z.jpg

Our Christmas trees are finally in! We have a large variety of types of trees including: Balsam fir, Douglas fir, Scotch pine, White pine, Blue spruce, and White spruce. They range in size from 5 feet to 10 feet. Come during store hours to pick out your favourite tree and we will have our employees load it up for you. First 100 trees sold will recieve one free strand of lights! Hurry on down while supplies last!

Christmas Parade

http://miltonsearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/milton-santa-claus-parade-460x250.jpg

Come join us this Saturday, December 6th for another amazing Christmas parade! Parade starts at 1:00 pm so make sure you arrive early to get a good view! After the parade there will be a meet and greet with Santa and Mrs. Claus in the arena. There is $5 ice skating available, but we don't rent out skates so don't forget to bring your own! There will be cookies and hot chocolate being handed out and lots of photo opportunities!
